Focus areas

We build our generative models using a technology called deep learning, which leverages large amounts of data to train an AI system to perform a task.

Introducing Whisper

We’ve trained and are open-sourcing a neural net that approaches human level robustness and accuracy on English speech recognition.

Jukebox

We’re introducing Jukebox, a neural net that generates music as raw audio in a variety of genres and artist styles.

MuseNet

We’ve created MuseNet, a deep neural network that can generate 4-minute musical compositions with 10 different instruments.
